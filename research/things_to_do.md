## ⭐ Popular Additions (Not in Sheet)

**Things to Do:**
* [**Noboribetsu Onsen & Hell Valley (Jigokudani)**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Noboribetsu+Onsen+Hell+Valley): Hokkaido's most famous hot spring town with steaming volcanic vents and mineral-rich baths. 
* [**Asahiyama Zoo**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Asahiyama+Zoo): Known for its interactive animal enclosures and famous penguin walks.
* [**Otaru Canal**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Otaru+Canal): A charming coastal area lined with old brick warehouses and soft lantern lights, highly recommended to combine with your Otaru day trip.

**Places to Eat:**
* [**Jingisukan Daruma Main Branch (Sapporo)**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Jingisukan+Daruma+Sapporo): A legendary spot open since 1954 for authentic, fresh grilled mutton (Genghis Khan).
* [**Kani Honke (Sapporo)**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kani+Honke+Sapporo): A legendary restaurant known for a full-on crab feast, serving hairy crabs flown directly from Tomakomai.
* [**Menya Saimi (Sapporo)**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Menya+Saimi+Sapporo): The gold standard for Sapporo Miso Ramen, often featuring a long line of locals.

## 💎 Hidden Gems (Off the Beaten Path)

**Things to Do:**
* [**Meiji Park Brick Silos (Nemuro City)**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Meiji+Park+Brick+Silos+Nemuro): Some of the largest and oldest surviving brick silos in Japan, set against lush nature for incredibly photogenic scenery.
* [**Hakodate Park Ferris Wheel**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Hakodate+Park+Ferris+Wheel): Japan's oldest operating Ferris wheel, offering a deep sense of nostalgia and history.
* [**Kamikawa Taisetsu Sake Brewery**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kamikawa+Taisetsu+Sake+Brewery): Try rare "Doburoku" (cloudy, unfiltered sake) in a small-scale, artisan rural brewery.

**Places to Eat:**
* [**Soup Curry Garaku (Sapporo)**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Soup+Curry+Garaku+Sapporo): Tucked away in an alley, this is the "quiet champion" of Sapporo soup curry.
* [**Uni Murakami (Hakodate)**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Uni+Murakami+Hakodate): A tiny counter near the morning market serving legendary sea urchin (Uni) donburi with catch sourced directly from Rishiri Island.
* [**Ramen Shishio (Asahikawa)**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Ramen+Shishio+Asahikawa): A 15-seat dive hidden off a snowy street serving incredible soy-based Shoyu Ramen.
* [**Kita no Aisu (Otaru)**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Kita+no+Aisu+Otaru): A hidden gem soft cream stand operating since 1978 near the canal, serving velvety, rich Hokkaido milk ice cream.
