## Notes

- **AVIS details:** Pick-up Jul 17 8:00am / Drop-off Jul 31 6:00pm / Pay at pickup
- **Drive CTS → Niseko:** ~2 hrs via Route 276 west. No toll road required; scenic mountain pass (Nakayama Pass) with views of Mt. Yotei on approach.
- **Check-in time:** Standard check-in is 3pm. Arriving at 10:30am is early — luggage storage is easy, hotel has facilities (pool, spa, café) to fill time.
- **E-bikes:** Available through the hotel or local rental shops in Hirafu. The Kutchan direction offers flat farmland roads with unobstructed Mt. Yotei views.
- **Zipline MACH3:** Located at Hanazono Resort (adjacent to hotel). Summer operating hours confirm availability in July. Book at hotel concierge or on arrival.
- **Energy caveat:** This is arrival day after an overnight transpacific flight (LAS → SFO → HND → CTS). Keep the afternoon loose — skip zipline if tired, save it for Day 2 morning.
- **Hirafu Village:** ~15 min from hotel. Best dinner concentration in Niseko — ramen, izakaya, pizza, and Japanese restaurants all walkable from each other.
