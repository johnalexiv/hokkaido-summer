## Notes

- **Book rafting in advance.** Saturday in July is peak season — morning slots fill quickly. Book directly with the operator or through hotel concierge.
- **Twin Peaks Bike Park:** Hanazono Resort runs a lift-accessed bike park in summer. Rental bikes available on site.
- **Hotel dinner options:** The Park Hyatt Niseko Hanazono has multiple F&B outlets. Teppanyaki is the most memorable; reserve ahead at check-in or by phone.
- **Pack light for rafting:** Operator provides wetsuits and gear. Bring a change of clothes and a dry bag for phone/wallet.
- **This is the last night at Park Hyatt.** Leave time in the evening to enjoy the terrace, hot spring bath, or just the quiet — checkout is tomorrow morning.
